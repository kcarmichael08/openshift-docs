[role="_abstract"]
You can configure {product-title-short} to send events to QRadar by configuring a generic webhook integration in {product-title-short}.

To configure your system:

. Configure the generic webhook.
. Configure policy notifications for the identifier.
. Configure QRadar to accept {product-title-short} events. See link:https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/dsm?topic=rhacsk-configuring-red-hat-advanced-cluster-security-kubernetes-communicate-qradar[Configuring Red Hat Advanced Cluster Security for Kubernetes to communicate with QRadar].

[NOTE]
====
When configuring the integration, in the *Endpoint* field, use the following example as a guide: `<URL to QRadar Box:<Port of Integration>`.
====
